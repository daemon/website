Hi, I'm Ralph.


I'm a lead research scientist at Comcast AI working on language technology. I publish with Ferhan Ture and Jimmy Lin in natural language processing, speech recognition, computer vision, and deep learning. My specific interests include model compression, explainable AI, probability theory, manifold learning, and geometric deep learning.

I've served as a reviewer for AAAI, AACL-IJCNLP, ACL, EACL, EMNLP, NAACL, and workshops at ACL, CVPR, ICML, and NeurIPS.
